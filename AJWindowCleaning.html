About Us.

Owned and operated by brothers Allux and Jakub Montoya, A&J Window Cleaning is a locally owned business based in Grantsville, Utah.

With 5 years of experience, A&J Window Cleaning has become a trusted name across Utah, from Tooele County and Salt Lake City to Ogden and Provo. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart, and we take pride in helping homes shine.

As a family-operated company, we understand the value of building lasting relationships with our clients by providing dependable, top-notch service. Whether you’re in the heart of Salt Lake City or nestled in the suburbs, A&J Window Cleaning is your go-to source for crystal-clear windows across Utah.

Why Choose Us?

For over 4 years, A&J Window Cleaning has been a trusted, family-owned business serving Tooele County with a commitment to quality, punctuality, and professionalism. Fully licensed and insured, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service that you can rely on. As a local business deeply rooted in the community, we understand the importance of providing meticulous work that meets the highest standards.
